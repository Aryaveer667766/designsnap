Frequently Asked Questions

What format will I receive the files in?

You will receive a fully editable PowerPoint file (.pptx). We can also provide a PDF version upon request at no extra charge.

What if I need revisions?

The Premium Tutor Service includes unlimited revisions. For per-page plans, one round of minor revisions is included to ensure you're satisfied.

How do I submit my notes and requirements?

After you reach out to us, you can simply email your requirements, handwritten notes, or documents to our dedicated project email address. We'll handle the rest!

Can you match the branding of my school?

Absolutely! Both of our service tiers include options for custom branded templates. Just send us your school's logo and color scheme, and we'll create presentations that match perfectly.
